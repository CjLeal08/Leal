    Palawan this is another island that is really famous here in the Philippines and palawan is also known as "the Last ecological Frontier" and the forests and the seas of Palawan are the natural home to impressive diversity and it's this kind of it's natural beauty that keeps the visitors coming to palawan. Palawan is a long and narrow island and it trends to the northeast-southwest between the south china sea and the sulu seas. The palawan tourist spot has been the talk of the world because of the beautiful charm of the island. Palawan is also known as the home of nature which palawan reserves both the sea, marine garden, giant sea clams, the sea turtles nesting on the white sand beaches and there are hundreds of species of butterflies, and lots of palm forest.
